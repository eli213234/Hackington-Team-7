This website is meant to introduce and educate a wide variety of people on different options when it comes to staying connected in a distributed world. There is undoubtedly a need for this, considering that there has been a significant change in the amount of digital communication due to the global pandemic. Bringing this information to people of different languages, cultures, backgrounds, and places is extreamly important if we wish to stay connected.
